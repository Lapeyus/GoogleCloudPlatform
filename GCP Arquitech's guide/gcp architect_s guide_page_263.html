The Drawbacks of Lift-and-Shift Migration


Lift-and-shift migration involves replicating an on-premises application setup in the cloud, which can lead to:


    

  • Higher costs compared to running a cloud-native app
    • 

  • Inability to fully utilize Google's services for monitoring, security, and governance
    • 

  • Potential performance issues due to compatibility problems
    • 



The Benefits of Refactoring


Refactoring, on the other hand, involves modifying an application to take advantage of cloud-native features. This approach offers significant benefits, including:


    

  • Enhanced performance and features to meet evolving business requirements
    • 

  • Improved integration with cloud-native security and a cloud database
    • 

  • Ability to fully utilize Google's services for monitoring, security, and governance
