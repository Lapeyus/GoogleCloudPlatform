Optimizing Network and Cost for Pricing in GCP


To optimize your network for pricing in Google Cloud Platform (GCP), it's essential to understand how traffic flows across your network and where you're placing your data.


Multi-Zone Design and Egress Traffic Charges


A multi-zone design is a best practice for resiliency and availability, but it comes with charges on egress traffic between zones within the same region. You can save money by deploying Google's CDN service or getting a dedicated network interconnect to GCP, which will save you 50% on your cost.


Egress Between Regions


Egressing between regions incurs standard egress charges as well as intercontinental egress charges, which can be expensive. Intercontinental egress can sometimes be more costly than standard egress.
